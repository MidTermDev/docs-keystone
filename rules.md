# Server Rules

By playing on Keystone, you agree to follow these rules. Violations result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans.

## General Rules

### 1. No Cheating
- No hacked clients
- No x-ray texture packs
- No auto-clickers or macros
- No fly hacks, speed hacks, etc.
- No kill aura or combat hacks

**Punishment**: Permanent ban

### 2. No Exploiting
- No duplicating items
- No exploiting bugs or glitches
- Report bugs, don't abuse them
- No abusing unintended mechanics

**Punishment**: Ban + removal of exploited items/money

### 3. No Real-Money Trading (RMT)
- Don't sell $KEY for real money outside official channels
- Don't buy accounts, items, or services for real money
- Don't advertise buying/selling

**Punishment**: Permanent ban

### 4. Be Respectful
- No harassment or bullying
- No hate speech or discrimination
- No excessive toxicity
- No personal attacks
- Trash talk in PVP is fine, but keep it game-related

**Punishment**: Mute → Temp ban → Perm ban

### 5. No Spam
- No chat spam or flooding
- No command spam
- No repetitive messages
- No excessive caps

**Punishment**: Mute

### 6. No Advertising
- No advertising other servers
- No posting random links
- No promoting outside services

**Punishment**: Mute → Ban

### 7. English in Global Chat
- Keep main chat in English
- Use party/faction chat for other languages
- This helps staff moderate

### 8. No Inappropriate Content
- No sexual content
- No excessive violence descriptions
- No disturbing content
- Keep builds appropriate

**Punishment**: Warning → Ban

## PVP Rules

### Allowed in PVP World
- Killing players
- Raiding bases
- Taking items from kills
- Camping (within reason)
- Trash talk (game-related)
- Alliances and betrayals

### NOT Allowed Anywhere
- Combat logging (punishment: items lost)
- Hacking in PVP
- DDoS threats
- Real-life threats
- Doxxing

## Economy Rules

### 1. No Scamming
- Honor trade agreements
- No bait-and-switch in shops
- No fake giveaways
- IRL trading scams = ban

**Punishment**: Items returned + temp ban → perm ban

### 2. No Market Manipulation
- No coordinated price fixing
- No artificial inflation schemes
- Normal competition is fine

### 3. No Economy Exploits
- Report dupes immediately
- Don't abuse economy bugs
- Exploiting = loss of all money + ban

## Building Rules

### In Survival World
- Don't build within 100 blocks of others without permission
- No offensive builds
- No lag machines
- Claim your builds

### In Resource World
- Don't build permanent structures
- World resets - builds will be deleted

### In PVP World
- Build at your own risk
- Can be raided/griefed
- No rules about base location

## Claim Rules

### GriefPrevention (Survival)
- Respect others' claims
- Don't claim around someone to trap them
- Abandoned claims may be removed

### Factions (PVP)
- Overclaiming is allowed when enemy is raidable
- Don't claim to grief (claim and never use)

## Chat Rules

### Do
- Be friendly
- Help new players
- Report rule breakers
- Use appropriate channels

### Don't
- Spam
- Harass
- Share personal information
- Impersonate staff
- Spread misinformation

## Staff Rules

### Staff Will
- Enforce rules fairly
- Help with issues
- Investigate reports
- Give warnings when appropriate

### Staff Won't
- Give free items
- Teleport you around
- Restore items from fair deaths
- Take sides in disputes

### Reporting Staff
If staff misbehaves, report to admins on Discord with evidence.

## Punishments

| Offense | First | Second | Third |
|---------|-------|--------|-------|
| Chat spam | Mute 10m | Mute 1h | Mute 1d |
| Harassment | Mute 1h | Temp ban 1d | Perm ban |
| Minor exploits | Warning | Temp ban 3d | Perm ban |
| Major exploits | Perm ban | - | - |
| Hacking | Perm ban | - | - |
| Scamming | Items returned + warning | Temp ban 7d | Perm ban |
| RMT | Perm ban | - | - |

## Appeals

Banned unfairly? Appeal on Discord.

Include:
- Your username
- What happened
- Why you should be unbanned
- Any evidence

Appeals are reviewed within 48 hours.

## Rule Changes

Rules may be updated. Major changes announced on Discord.

"I didn't know" is not an excuse - check rules regularly.
