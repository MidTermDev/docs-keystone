# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Keystone?
Keystone is a Minecraft survival server where you earn $KEY cryptocurrency by playing. Mine, hunt, farm, and fight to earn real tokens on the Solana blockchain.

### What version of Minecraft do I need?
Java Edition 1.21.4

### Is Keystone free to play?
Yes, completely free. No purchases required. You earn by playing.

### Is this pay-to-win?
No. There's no way to buy advantages. Everyone earns through gameplay.

---

## Economy Questions

### What is $KEY?
$KEY is a Solana SPL token. It's the currency of Keystone with real-world value.

### How do I earn $KEY?
1. Join a job (`/jobs browse`)
2. Do activities (mine, hunt, farm, etc.)
3. Get paid automatically

### How much can I earn?
Depends on your activity and job levels:
- New players: Up to 2,000 KEY/hour
- Max level players: Up to 5,000 KEY/hour

### How do I withdraw $KEY?
1. Link your wallet: `/linkwallet <solana-address>`
2. Withdraw: `/withdraw <amount>`
3. Check your Solana wallet

### Is there a minimum withdrawal?
Yes, 100 KEY minimum.

### Why can't I use /sell?
We disabled NPC selling to prevent dupe exploits. Trade with players using ChestShop instead.

### Where do I trade items?
At the Market (`/warp market`) or create shops anywhere you can build.

## Jobs Questions

### How many jobs can I have?
Up to 3 at the same time.

### What happens if I leave a job?
You lose 30% of your levels in that job.

### What's the best job?
Depends on playstyle:
- **Miner** - Consistent, good for grinding
- **Hunter** - Fun, variety of mobs
- **Raider** - High risk/reward PVP
- **Farmer** - Passive, good for AFK-lite play

### Why am I not getting paid?
Check:
- You have the job (`/jobs stats`)
- You haven't hit hourly limit
- You're not near a mob spawner
- You're breaking the right blocks

### Do spawner farms work?
No. Payment near spawners is disabled to prevent AFK exploitation.

## World Questions

### What worlds are there?
- **Spawn** - Hub with portals
- **Survival** - Building, permanent
- **Resource** - Mining, resets weekly
- **PVP** - Factions, full PVP
- **Nether/End** - Dimensions

### Does the resource world reset?
Yes, weekly. Don't build there.

### Is PVP everywhere?
No. PVP is off at spawn and in claims (Survival). It's on in Resource and PVP worlds.

### Can I lose items when I die?
- **Spawn**: You can't die
- **Survival**: Keep inventory ON
- **Resource**: Keep inventory ON
- **PVP World**: Keep inventory OFF (you drop items)

## Factions Questions

### What are factions?
Player groups that claim land, build bases, and raid enemies in the PVP world.

### How do I create a faction?
`/f create <name>` - Costs 500 KEY

### How does power work?
- Each player has 0-20 power
- Power = chunks you can claim
- Die = lose 5 power
- Power regenerates over time

### When can I be raided?
When your faction power drops below your number of claims.

### Is betrayal allowed?
Technically yes, but extreme cases may be punished.

## Shop Questions

### How do I create a shop?
1. Place a chest
2. Put a sign on it:
   ```
   [blank]
   64
   B 100 : S 50
   DIAMOND
   ```
3. Stock the chest

### What does B and S mean?
- B = Price others BUY from you
- S = Price others SELL to you

### Where can I make shops?
- Market (`/warp market`)
- Your claimed land
- Your faction territory

### Why isn't my shop working?
- Check sign format
- Make sure chest has items
- Verify item name is correct

## Technical Questions

### I'm lagging, what do I do?
1. Lower render distance
2. Close other programs
3. Check your internet
4. Try reconnecting

### I got kicked for "flying"
Anti-cheat false positive. Just reconnect. If persistent, contact staff in-game.

### My stuff disappeared!
Contact staff with:
- What happened
- When it happened
- What you lost
Staff can check logs and potentially restore items.

### How do I report a bug?
Contact staff in-game or DM us on Twitter [@KeystoneP2E](https://x.com/KeystoneP2E) with:
- Description
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots if possible

## Account Questions

### How do I link my wallet?
`/linkwallet <your-solana-address>`

Use a wallet like Phantom or Solflare.

### Can I change my linked wallet?
Contact staff - requires verification.

### What if I lose my wallet?
We cannot recover lost wallets or their contents. Keep your seed phrase safe.

### Can I transfer my account?
No. Accounts are non-transferable.
