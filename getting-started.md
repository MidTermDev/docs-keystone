# Getting Started

This guide walks you through your first hour on Keystone.

## Step 1: Join the Server

1. Launch Minecraft Java Edition (1.21.4)
2. Click "Multiplayer" â†’ "Add Server"
3. Enter server address: `play.keystone.gg`
4. Click "Join Server"

## Step 2: Spawn Area

When you join, you'll spawn at the central hub. Look around - you'll see:

- **Welcome hologram** with basic info
- **4 colored paths** leading to portals
- **Info board** with server guide
- **4 tutorial signs** around the center

### The Portals

| Direction | Color | Portal | Description |
|-----------|-------|--------|-------------|
| North | Cyan | Resource World | Mining & gathering |
| East | Green | Survival World | Building & exploring |
| South | Red | PVP World | Factions & raiding |
| West | Yellow | Market | Player shops |

## Step 3: Get Your Starter Kit

Type this command:

```
/kit starter
```

You'll receive:
- Stone sword
- Stone pickaxe
- Stone axe
- 16 bread
- 32 torches
- 16 oak logs

## Step 4: Choose a Job

Jobs are how you earn $KEY. Open the jobs menu:

```
/jobs browse
```

A GUI will open showing available jobs. Click one to see details, then click again to join.

**Recommended first jobs:**

| Job | Best For | Easy Money From |
|-----|----------|-----------------|
| Miner | Mining fans | Breaking ores |
| Hunter | Combat lovers | Killing mobs |
| Farmer | Peaceful players | Harvesting crops |

You can have up to **3 jobs** at once.

## Step 5: Pick a World

Walk to a portal based on your playstyle:

### Resource World (North - Cyan)
- Go here to **mine and gather**
- World resets periodically (your items stay)
- Best for: Miner job, getting materials

### Survival World (East - Green)
- Go here to **build a base**
- Permanent world, your builds stay forever
- Best for: Building, farming, peaceful play

### PVP World (South - Red)
- Go here for **factions and raiding**
- Full PVP enabled, can lose items
- Best for: Raider job, competitive players

## Step 6: Start Earning

Once you have a job and you're in a world, just play normally:

- **Miner**: Break stone and ores
- **Hunter**: Kill mobs
- **Farmer**: Harvest crops
- **Woodcutter**: Chop trees

You'll see payment notifications as you earn $KEY.

## Step 7: Check Your Balance

```
/balance
```

or just:

```
/bal
```

This shows your current $KEY balance.

## Step 8: Set a Home

Found a nice spot? Set your home:

```
/sethome
```

Return anytime with:

```
/home
```

## Step 9: Return to Spawn

Need to get back to spawn?

```
/spawn
```

Or walk through a **blue return portal** in any world.

## Tips for New Players

1. **Don't rush to PVP world** - Gear up in Survival first
2. **Join multiple jobs** - You can have 3, mix and match
3. **Set homes** - You get more home slots as you rank up
4. **Check the market** - Other players sell useful items
5. **Read signs** - Tutorial info is posted around spawn
